Welcome to Yar!

The card game of strategy and luck.
Choose a mode to begin:

Scoring Categories:

Ones (Ace): Sum of all ones
Twos: Sum of all twos
Threes: Sum of all threes
Fours: Sum of all fours
Fives: Sum of all fives
Sixes: Sum of all sixes
Duo: Sum for two of a kind
Triple: Sum for three of a kind
Quad: Sum for four of a kind
Den o Wolf: Score for two pairs
Dragon Claw: Score for a full house (three of a kind + a pair)
Yar: Score for five of a kind
Straight: Score for a sequence of five cards
Full House: Score for three of a kind and a pair
Chance: Sum of all cards
Total: Sum of all your locked scores
